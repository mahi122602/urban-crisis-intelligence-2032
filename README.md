# AI-Powered Urban Crisis Intelligence System for Brisbane 2032

## Overview

The AI-Powered Urban Crisis Intelligence System is a smart-city analytics platform designed to simulate, monitor, and predict operational risks during the Brisbane 2032 Olympic and Paralympic Games.

This project combines:
- predictive analytics,
- geospatial intelligence,
- real-time weather integration,
- explainable AI,
- operational alerts,
- and interactive dashboards

to support city-scale crisis monitoring and decision-making.

---

# Key Features

## AI Crisis Prediction Engine
Predicts:
- Low Risk
- Medium Risk
- High Risk
- Critical Risk

using machine learning models trained on simulated Olympic operational scenarios.

---

## Real-Time Weather Intelligence
Integrates live Brisbane weather data using Open-Meteo API:
- temperature
- humidity
- rainfall
- wind speed

to enhance climate-risk awareness.

---

## Brisbane GIS Intelligence Map
Interactive geospatial dashboard displaying:
- venue risk zones
- operational hotspots
- crisis severity indicators

across Brisbane Olympic venues.

---

## Explainable AI
Displays feature importance to explain:
- what factors most influence crisis prediction,
- and how the AI model makes decisions.

---

## Real-Time Crisis Alerts
Automatically detects:
- extreme heat conditions
- crowd congestion risks
- transport disruption risks
- accessibility concerns
- critical operational scenarios
